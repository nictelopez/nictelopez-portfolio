The process of working with me .

What can I offer you.

I will develop a website for your business that meets your expectations and your business needs. I know that having communication during the process is important for things to work well, that's why I present you the steps to follow when creating your project.

  1. Consultation: Where all begins. Starting with a conversation where we will be talking about your project.
  2. Proposal: I will be delivering you a proposal about your project, where I'll be explaining in detail the process of creating your web page.
  3. Development: The stage where I'll be working on creating your project, while you will be receiving updates periodically about it.
  4. Deploy: Final step. The moment where I will deliver to you in time and form the results of the web page.
